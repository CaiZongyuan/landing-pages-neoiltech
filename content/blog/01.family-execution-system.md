# Family execution is the missing system

Modern households already have information everywhere. What they rarely have is a system that takes responsibility for outcomes.

## The real gap

- Messages are fragmented across apps.
- One person still remembers everything.
- Follow-through breaks when context is scattered.

## What AstraFlow does

AstraFlow turns fragmented inputs into coordinated action. It keeps household context, routes work to the right agent, and follows through until the task is complete.

## Why this matters

When a family depends on one invisible operator, every missed detail becomes stress. A Family Execution System distributes memory, responsibility, and recovery.
