WHY NOW
Why now

The problem is no longer “how do we record family life?” The real gap is execution ownership across fragmented tools, schedules, and obligations.

Invisible household labor keeps one person overloaded

The household operator is still the system of last resort: remembering, nudging, checking, escalating, and absorbing the cost of every miss.

Current tools solve slices, not the full loop

Messaging, calendars, to-dos, and vertical apps each handle one surface. None of them owns who should act, what is blocked, and what needs escalation.

The missing piece is execution ownership

AstraFlow gives families a system that routes work, keeps state, and moves things to completion instead of leaving everything inside someone’s head.

HOW ASTRAFLOW WORKS
How AstraFlow works

AstraFlow continuously builds family context, routes decisions to the right agent, confirms sensitive actions, and compounds what the household learns over time.

OPERATING LOOP
Capture → Structure → Route → Execute → Follow Through → Reflect → Build Context
01
Capture + Structure

Household Gateway gathers messages, schedules, notes, and context into a shared operating layer.

02
Route to the right agent

Personalized AI Agents understand identity, permissions, priorities, and which member should act next.

03
Execute with the right level of control

Sensitive steps stay permissioned. Routine coordination can move forward without chaos.

04
Follow through and build context

Each outcome sharpens future routing and turns repeated work into reusable EaseFlows.
